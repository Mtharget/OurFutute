About Ikusasalethu Secondary School

Ikusasalethu Secondary School is dedicated to fostering an environment of excellence where learners are empowered to achieve their academic and personal best. Our school promotes holistic development, focusing on nurturing future leaders who are prepared to contribute positively to society.

Vision

To provide quality education to learners in order to uplift and empower them to play responsible and meaningful role in society

Mission

Encourage parental and community involvement in our school.Raise standards to learners at all level both in out of the school.provide quality education by means of instruction, guidance and facilitation by a care of qualified, dedicated and accountable educators.

Mission Statement

We the community of Ikusasalethu commit ourselves to provide excellent & relavant education to our learners by working in the highest proffesional standards through dedication, discipline and commitment in order to develop responsible and capable members of the society.

Core Values

  • Honesty
  • Loyalty
  • Respect
  • Self-Discipline
  • Sociability
  • Responsibility
